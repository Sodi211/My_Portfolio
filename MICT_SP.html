MICT SETA Dashboard

Project Overview

This interactive Power BI dashboard was developed to analyze and visualize training data for MICT SETA (Media, Information and Communication Technologies Sector Education and Training Authority).

Key Features

  • Interactive filters for year, province, and training type
  • Visualizations of training trends over time
  • Geographical distribution of training programs
  • Demographic breakdown of beneficiaries

Technologies Used

Power BI DAX SQL Data Modeling ETL
